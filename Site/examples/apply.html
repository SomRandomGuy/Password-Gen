Civil Protection

Undertake in the protection of your community, and work toward unity within our society while collaborating with other like-minded subjects.

Perks

The perks of being a Civil Protection Officer are as follows:

Highly Increased quality/quantity of rations

Highly Increased quality of living quarters

Highest security clearance

No inspections

Usage of advanced equipment provided by Our Benefactors

Priority access to public transportation

Increase in authority

More privileges wil be disclosed shortly after joining


Apply

Infestation Control

Engage in cleanout operations to fight against the highly dangerous invasive species which infiltrated our world due to the catastrophic human failures at Black Mesa, which, If not contained in the coming years, it will pose a significant threat to you and the community. To combat this looming threat, you will be assigned to perform standard cleanout protocols within areas of high priority within and beyond your sector. On occasion, you will collobrate with the personnel of the Engineer Core to efficiently combat the infection.

Perks

Advantages of collabaration in Infestation Control are the following:

Increased quality/quantity of rations

Increased quality of living quarters

Improved security clearance

Less inspections

Usage of advanced equipment provided by Our Benefactors

Priority access to public transportation


Apply

To collaborate, locate your local recruitment station.

Engineer Core

Engage in various engineering related tasks within and and beyond your designated sector. Such tasks may include the construction or maintenance of any existing facilities on the request of Our Benefactors. You might also be tasked to participate in the maintenance of Old World facilities, such as apartment blocks and public spaces. On rare occasion, you will be tasked to collaborate with Infestation Control to assist in cleanout operations.
