District 10 Citadel Completed

After years of hard work by the personnel of Engineer Core Group 21, in collaboration with other construction personnel, have completed the District 10 Citadel. Any associated personnel of this project, as noted by Our Benefactors, will be issued the following rewards for their service:

Increased quality/quantity of rations

Enchanced quality of living quarters

Upgraded security clearance

Priority access to public transportation


To earn similar benefits and contribute to the efficiency of our society, visit the apply page and join the Engineering Brigade.

New Dress Polcies

Effective August 12th, new dress policies will commence. Every citizen will be required to be in possession of the following attire:

Black Footwear (size change available on request)

Blue Trousers (size change available on request)

Blue Tunic w/citizen nametag


After August 17th, every subject not in possession of the garments will be subject to the following penalties:

Decreased rations

Reduced clearance level


Visit your local Distribution Center to obtain your accoutrements, or be subject to the former sanctions. Thank you for your compliance.

Several Anti-Citizens Captured

In a strategic operation executed by Civil Protection Forces, a hideout accommodating subversive illegal contraband has been thoroughly investigated, resulting in the detention of five Anti-Citizens. The individuals have been charged with the following offences:

Possession of contraband literature

Possession of prohibited media

Possession of unauthorized digital media


After a thorough evaluation, those subjects have been relocated off world to await further instruction and deployment for their duties.
